Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development process, pricing, and how we help your business grow online.

1. How long does it take to build a website?

+

Most standard business websites are completed within 5–10 days. However, the exact timeline depends entirely on the complexity of your project, the number of pages, and specific custom features. Once we finalize the requirements and receive the 20% advance token, our team works efficiently to deliver a premium, bug-free website well within the promised deadline.

2. Do I need to provide content and images?

+

Ideally, yes! Nobody knows your business better than you. However, if you don't have content ready, you don't need to worry. We can assist you by providing basic, SEO-optimized content and sourcing high-quality, royalty-free premium images that perfectly match your brand's aesthetic.

3. Will my website work seamlessly on mobile phones?

+

Absolutely! In today's digital age, over 70% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. We follow a strict "Mobile-First" design approach. Every website we build is fully responsive, ensuring it looks stunning and functions smoothly across all mobile phones, tablets, and desktop screens.

4. Can I update my website after it’s completed?

+

Yes, you maintain full control over your content! If you opt for our Business or Premium plans, we integrate a powerful Content Management System (CMS) like WordPress. This allows you or your team to easily update text, change images, and add new blog posts anytime, without needing any coding knowledge.

5. Do you provide domain and hosting services?

+

We do not directly sell domain and hosting because we believe you should have 100% ownership of your digital assets. However, we will guide you step-by-step to purchase the best and most secure domain and hosting for your specific needs. Additionally, we provide free Cloudflare setup to ensure your site is blazing fast and secure.

6. Will my website be SEO-friendly to rank on Google?

+

Yes, all our websites are engineered with a strong technical SEO foundation. We ensure clean code, lightning-fast load times, proper heading structures (H1, H2, H3), and mobile optimization. This basic-to-advanced SEO structure gives your website the best possible chance to rank higher on Google search results.

7. What if I need changes after the website is delivered?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority. After the final delivery, we provide free technical support (ranging from 7 to 30 days, depending on your chosen plan). During this period, we will happily assist you with minor design tweaks, content updates, and any bug fixes at zero additional cost.

8. Do you offer custom designs or just use templates?

+

We do not believe in cookie-cutter solutions. Webnex prides itself on creating modern, highly customized websites tailored specifically to your brand identity and business goals. From elegant glassmorphism UI to dark-themed corporate designs, we build what reflects your vision.

9. How do I get started with my project?

+

Getting started is incredibly easy! Simply click on the “Get Started” button or reach out to us directly via WhatsApp. We will schedule a quick consultation to understand your requirements, provide a transparent quote, and once the 20% token is processed, our development phase begins immediately.

10. What makes Webnex different from other developers?

+

We bridge the gap between expensive corporate agencies and unreliable freelancers. Webnex focuses on providing Institutional-Grade Premium Design, Lightning-Fast Delivery, and Highly Affordable Pricing. We maintain absolute transparency throughout the process, ensuring your digital presence actively helps your business grow.
