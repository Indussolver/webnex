Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development process, pricing, and how we help your business grow online.

1. How long does it take to build a website?

+

Most standard business websites are completed within 5–10 days. However, the exact timeline depends entirely on the complexity of your project, the number of pages, and specific custom features. Once we finalize the requirements and receive the 20% advance token, our team works efficiently to deliver a premium, bug-free website well within the promised deadline.

2. Do I need to provide content and images?

+

Ideally, yes! Nobody knows your business better than you. However, if you don't have content ready, you don't need to worry. We can assist you by providing basic, SEO-optimized content and sourcing high-quality, royalty-free premium images that perfectly match your brand's aesthetic.

3. Will my website work seamlessly on mobile phones?

+

Absolutely! In today's digital age, over 70% of web traffic comes from mobile devices. We follow a strict "Mobile-First" design approach. Every website we build is fully responsive, ensuring it looks stunning and functions smoothly across all mobile phones, tablets, and desktop screens.

4. Can I update my website after it’s completed?

+

Yes, you maintain full control over your content! If you opt for our Business or Premium plans, we integrate a powerful Content Management System (CMS) like WordPress. This allows you or your team to easily update text, change images, and add new blog posts anytime, without needing any coding knowledge.

5. Do you provide domain and hosting services?

+

We do not directly sell domain and hosting because we believe you should have 100% ownership of your digital assets. However, we will guide you step-by-step to purchase the best and most secure domain and hosting for your specific needs. Additionally, we provide free Cloudflare setup to ensure your site is blazing fast and secure.

6. Will my website be SEO-friendly to rank on Google?

+

Yes, all our websites are engineered with a strong technical SEO foundation. We ensure clean code, lightning-fast load times, proper heading structures (H1, H2, H3), and mobile optimization. This basic-to-advanced SEO structure gives your website the best possible chance to rank higher on Google search results.

7. What if I need changes after the website is delivered?

+

Your satisfaction is our priority. After the final delivery, we provide free technical support (ranging from 7 to 30 days, depending on your chosen plan). During this period, we will happily assist you with minor design tweaks, content updates, and any bug fixes at zero additional cost.

8. Do you offer custom designs or just use templates?

+

We do not believe in cookie-cutter solutions. Webnex prides itself on creating modern, highly customized websites tailored specifically to your brand identity and business goals. From elegant glassmorphism UI to dark-themed corporate designs, we build what reflects your vision.

9. How do I get started with my project?

+

Getting started is incredibly easy! Simply click on the “Get Started” button or reach out to us directly via WhatsApp. We will schedule a quick consultation to understand your requirements, provide a transparent quote, and once the 20% token is processed, our development phase begins immediately.

10. What makes Webnex different from other developers?

+

We bridge the gap between expensive corporate agencies and unreliable freelancers. Webnex focuses on providing Institutional-Grade Premium Design, Lightning-Fast Delivery, and Highly Affordable Pricing. We maintain absolute transparency throughout the process, ensuring your digital presence actively helps your business grow.

11. Do you redesign existing websites?

+

Yes, we breathe new life into outdated websites. We analyze your current site and rebuild it with a modern glassmorphism or minimalist UI, ensuring better conversion rates, improved mobile responsiveness, and overall premium performance.

12. Will my website be fast loading?

+

Speed is critical for user retention. We optimize image sizes, minify code, and utilize premium caching tools (like Cloudflare) to ensure your website loads in under 3 seconds, providing a seamless experience for your visitors.

13. Can you create an e-commerce website?

+

Absolutely. We can build robust online stores using industry-standard platforms. Depending on your requirements, we set up product catalogs, payment gateways, inventory management, and a seamless checkout experience (extra costs apply for e-commerce logic).

14. Do you provide logo design as well?

+

While our core expertise is web development, we provide basic typography-based logos to get you started. For highly illustrative or complex corporate branding, we can guide you to professional logo designers within our trusted network.

15. Will my website be secure?

+

Yes. We implement fundamental security practices, including SSL certificates (HTTPS), secure form validations, and basic firewall configurations to keep your business and customer data safe from common online threats.

16. Can I connect my social media accounts?

+

Definitely. We seamlessly integrate your Instagram feeds, Facebook links, direct WhatsApp chat widgets, and LinkedIn profiles directly into your website's header, footer, or contact sections to boost your social engagement.

17. Do you provide content writing?

+

We assist with basic, SEO-friendly foundational content to structure your website. However, for highly specialized, persuasive, and industry-specific copywriting, we recommend collaborating with a dedicated professional copywriter.

18. What payment methods do you accept?

+

For maximum convenience, we accept all major payment methods including UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), direct Bank Transfers (NEFT/IMPS), and secure online credit/debit card payments.

19. Do I need technical knowledge to manage my website?

+

Not at all! If your site is built on a CMS, we provide a very user-friendly dashboard. We also offer a quick handover session so you can easily manage text and images without ever touching a single line of code.

20. Will my website appear on Google?

+

Yes. Beyond basic SEO, we ensure your website is properly structured so it can be indexed by Google Search Console. We set up the necessary sitemaps and meta tags so search engines can easily discover your pages.

21. Can I request changes during development?

+

Transparency is key to our process. We allow structured, basic revisions during the development phase. We keep you updated at various milestones so you can provide feedback before the final launch.

22. Do you offer support after 30 days?

+

Yes, we do. While initial post-launch support is free, we offer highly affordable Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) or hourly-rate extended support for long-term updates, backups, and technical assistance.

23. Can you create websites for international clients?

+

Absolutely. We operate globally and have successfully delivered high-performing websites for clients across the USA, Europe, Africa, Latin America, and Asia, easily adapting to local time zones for communication.

24. Will my website have a contact form?

+

Yes, every website we build includes a fully functional, secure contact form that routes leads directly to your email inbox, alongside direct click-to-chat WhatsApp integration for instant client communication.

25. Do you provide hosting setup support?

+

Yes. While you purchase and own the hosting, we handle the entire complex technical setup. We connect your domain, configure the DNS records, install the SSL certificate, and securely deploy the website to your server.

26. Can I upgrade my plan later?

+

Scalability is built into our designs. You can start with a Starter or Business plan and easily upgrade to an Elite package later to add features like e-commerce, automated CRMs, or advanced animations as your business grows.

27. Will my website be unique?

+

Yes. We don't use rigid, overused templates. We focus on custom layouts, bespoke color palettes, and modern UI trends (like dark mode and glassmorphism) tailored exclusively to your brand's unique identity.

28. Do you provide backup for websites?

+

Data safety is crucial. We provide basic backup guidance and can install automated backup plugins for CMS websites, ensuring you always have a secure copy of your site in case of unexpected server emergencies.

29. Can you integrate Google Maps?

+

Yes, especially for local businesses! We embed interactive Google Maps directly onto your Contact page or Footer section, making it incredibly easy for local customers to navigate to your physical store or office.

30. What if I don’t like the design?

+

Client satisfaction is our ultimate goal. We share early wireframes and design mockups before full coding begins. We offer dedicated revision options to ensure the final product perfectly aligns with your initial vision.
