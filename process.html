How we bring your Vision to Life

No confusion, no hidden fees. Just a clear, 5-step journey from your first message to a fully live, Google-listed business website.

1
Project Kickoff

Booking & Details

We start by understanding your business, design preferences, and goals. To lock in your project, we take a nominal 20% booking token. Once received, our design work officially begins.

2
First Look

Demo Presentation

Within a few days, we will present a live working demo of your website. You get to see the colors, layout, and structure exactly as your customers will see it.

3
Refinement

Feedback & Improvements

Your satisfaction is our priority. You tell us what changes or improvements you need in the demo, and our team will tweak the design until it perfectly matches your vision.

4
Finalization

Hosting & 80% Handover

You decide: buy your own premium Domain/Hosting (subscription required), or use our Free Cloudflare Hosting. After clearing the remaining 80% payment, we hand over full admin access and provide your entire source code in a ZIP file.

5
Go Live

Google Listing & Live Setup

Regardless of your hosting choice, we will officially list your new website on Google. Your business is now digital, searchable, and ready to welcome new customers!

Client Preparation

What We Need From You 💬

Starting your website is simpler than you think. You don’t need technical skills or complicated preparation — just a few basic details to get things moving.

🏢

Your Business Information

Tell us about your business — what you do, who you serve, and what makes you unique. This helps us design a website that truly represents your brand.

🎨

Logo (Optional)

If you already have a logo, great — we’ll use it. If not, no worries at all. We can work with a clean text-based design or guide you with simple options.

📝

Content (Or We Can Help)

You can provide your own content (text, services, descriptions), or simply share basic details — we’ll help structure and write it professionally for you.

📞

Contact Details

Share the information your customers should use to reach you: Phone number, Email address, Physical address (if applicable), and Social media links.

💡

That’s It — We Handle the Rest

Once we have these basics, our team takes over — designing, building, and refining your website step-by-step. No stress. No technical headaches. Just a smooth, guided process from start to finish.
